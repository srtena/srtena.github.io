PRESS KIT | TECHNICAL RIDER

Hailing from the north of Spain and the idyllic coasts of Vanuatu, Baywaves were born as a duo and quickly created a new musical genre they named «hypnopop». The work wasn’t finished until their moving to Madrid, where they became a quartet. As a full band, Baywaves gained attention from international media with their first single «The Freak Kingdom», which was featured in blogs over the world and let them win several new music contests in their country.

After this strong breakout, Baywaves release now their debut EP “Only For Uz” (Foehn Records, 2016), an impressive collection of shimmering, fuzzy and reverb-drenched psych pop tunes that will enhance anyone’s post-summer depression. With this release, the band will be showcasing their music live in a big tour that will lead them to festivals such as Primavera Sound, FIB Benicàssim, Cara-B or SOS 4.8.
